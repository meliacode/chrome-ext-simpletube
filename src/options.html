General Options

Turn on simple cleanup controls to hide common distractions in feeds and subscription views, without changing your YouTube account data or history.

Video Length Filter

Choose a minimum and maximum video length (in minutes), then decide whether videos outside that range should be faded or fully hidden.

Lower bound in minutes. Videos shorter than this are treated as out of range. Default: 0 minutes
Upper bound in minutes. Videos longer than this are treated as out of range. Default: 30 minutes
Controls the visual effect for out-of-range videos: Fade keeps them visible but dim, Hide removes them from layout. Impact: Hide is stricter than Fade.

Video Views Filter

Choose a preferred views range (in number of views), then decide whether videos outside that range should be faded or fully hidden.

Lower bound for visible view counts. Videos below this are treated as out of range. Default: 0
Upper bound for visible view counts. Videos above this are treated as out of range. Default: 1M
Controls the visual effect for out-of-range videos: Fade keeps them visible but dim, Hide removes them from layout. Impact: unrecognized YouTube view formats are left unchanged.
