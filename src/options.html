General Options

Reduce common distractions across YouTube pages without changing your subscriptions or history.

Video Length Filter

Set a preferred duration range. Videos outside the range can be faded or hidden completely.

Videos shorter than this value will be affected. Default: 0 minutes
Videos longer than this value will be affected. Default: 30 minutes
Fade keeps non-matching videos visible. Hide removes them from view. Impact: Hide is stricter than Fade.

Video Views Filter

Control how much popularity matters by fading or hiding videos outside your preferred views range.

Videos below this number will be affected. Default: 0
Videos above this number will be affected. Default: 1M
Fade gives softer filtering. Hide produces a stricter, shorter list. Impact: unrecognized YouTube view formats are left unchanged.

Subscription Categories

Organize subscribed channels into categories so your subscriptions page is easier to filter. Category assignments are managed from the channels listing page .

Keep names short so category tabs stay readable on the subscriptions page.

Saved categories

    Impact: deleting a category also removes that assignment from subscribed channels using it.
